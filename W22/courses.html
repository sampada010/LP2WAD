Courses Offered at PICT

Undergraduate Programs

  • B.E. in Computer Engineering

    4-year program focusing on computer hardware, software, networking, and system design.

  • B.E. in Information Technology

    4-year program covering IT infrastructure, database systems, and network management.

  • B.E. in Electronics & Telecommunication

    4-year program on electronic devices, communication systems, and signal processing.

Postgraduate Programs

  • M.Tech in Computer Engineering

    2-year advanced program with specializations in AI, Machine Learning, and Big Data.

  • M.Tech in Information Technology

    2-year advanced program with focus on cloud computing, IoT, and cybersecurity.

Doctoral Programs

  • Ph.D. in Computer Engineering

    Research program in various domains of computer science and engineering.

  • Ph.D. in Electronics & Telecommunication

    Research program focusing on advanced communication technologies.

Certificate Courses

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

    6-month intensive program covering ML algorithms, neural networks, and applications.

  • Cybersecurity

    4-month program on network security, ethical hacking, and security protocols.

  • Full Stack Web Development

    3-month intensive program covering frontend and backend technologies.

Admission Process

Admissions to PICT are based on:

  • For UG: JEE Mains / MHT-CET scores followed by counseling
  • For PG: GATE scores and academic performance
  • For Ph.D.: Research proposal evaluation and interview

For detailed admission procedures, please contact the admission office.
