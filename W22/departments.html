Academic Departments

Computer Engineering Department

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in 1983 and has been at the forefront of computer education in the region. The department boasts state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty members.

Facilities:

  • High-Performance Computing Lab
  • AI and Machine Learning Lab
  • Software Development Center
  • Internet of Things Lab

Research Areas:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Distributed Computing
  • Data Science and Analytics

Information Technology Department

Established in 1999, the IT Department focuses on providing quality education in information technology, network management, and database systems. The department has close ties with the IT industry.

Facilities:

  • Database Management Lab
  • Network Security Lab
  • Web Technology Center
  • Cloud Computing Lab

Research Areas:

  • Cloud Computing and Virtualization
  • Cybersecurity and Cryptography
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Internet of Things (IoT)

Electronics & Telecommunication Department

The E&TC Department offers comprehensive programs covering electronic circuits, communication systems, and signal processing techniques. The department has well-equipped laboratories for practical training.

Facilities:

  • Digital Signal Processing Lab
  • VLSI Design Lab
  • Microprocessor and Microcontroller Lab
  • Communication Systems Lab

Research Areas:

  • Wireless Communication
  • VLSI Design and Testing
  • Embedded Systems
  • Signal and Image Processing

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

The offered curriculum is interdisciplinary in nature by fine tuning computer science discipline with Mathematical Foundations, Artificial Intelligence Techniques, Machine Learning Principles, Statistical Methods, Data Science principles and methodologies.

Facilities:

  • Holistic student learning environment
  • Well-equipped laboratories
  • Strong PICT alumni base

Focus Areas:

  • Research & development
  • Academic Collaboration
  • Data Analysis
  • Professional Ethics

Electronics and Computer Engineering

The main research focus of the ECE is in the areas of Embedded Design, Semiconductor Devices, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cryptography, Network Security, Parallel-Distributed and High-Performance Computing, Algorithms, and Cloud Computing, etc.

Facilities:

  • Holistic student learning environment
  • Well-equipped laboratories
  • Well trained and qualified supporting technical staff members

Focus Areas:

  • Industry-Institution Interactions and collaborative for R&D activities
  • Academic Collaboration
  • Multi-disciplinary courses in emerging areas
  • Publish good quality paper publications in refereed journals.
