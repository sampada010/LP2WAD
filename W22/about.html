Our Story

Established in 1983, Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is one of the premier engineering institutes in Maharashtra, affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University and approved by AICTE.

The institute was established by the Society for Computer Technology and Research (SCTR) to develop skilled professionals in the field of Computer Engineering and Information Technology.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education and research, producing globally competent and socially responsible professionals.

Mission

  • To provide quality education through innovative teaching-learning processes
  • To create professionals with strong technical foundation and ethical values
  • To promote research culture among faculty and students
  • To establish strong industry-institute interaction

Achievements

  • Consistently high rankings in engineering college surveys
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure and laboratories
  • Strong placement records with leading tech companies
  • Numerous research publications by faculty and students
  • NAAC accredited with 'A' grade

Campus Facilities

  • Advanced Computing Labs
  • Digital Library with e-resources
  • Seminar Halls and Auditorium
  • Sports Complex
  • Wi-Fi Enabled Campus
