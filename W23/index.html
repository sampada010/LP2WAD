Robotics Club

The PICT Robotics Club offers students an opportunity to explore robotics technology and develop practical skills in designing, building, and programming robots. The club organizes workshops, competitions, and project-based learning activities to enhance students' understanding of mechanical engineering, electronics, and programming.

Achievements

  • National Robotics Competition 2024 - First Prize
  • Inter-College Tech Fest 2023 - Best Innovation Award
  • Successfully designed and built an autonomous rescue robot for disaster management
  • Represented PICT at IIT Bombay's Techfest 2023

Pictoreal

Pictoreal is PICT's premier photography and media club, focusing on capturing moments and telling stories through visual media. Members learn various photography techniques, photo editing, videography, and graphic design. The club handles event coverage and maintains the visual documentation of college activities.

Achievements

  • Published annual magazine 'Frames' featuring top photography work
  • Photography Exhibition 2024 - Displayed 100+ photographs to public audience
  • Winner of the State Level Visual Storytelling Competition 2023
  • Successfully conducted photography workshops for over 300 students

PICT ACM Student Chapter (PASC)

PASC is the official ACM student chapter of PICT, focusing on computer science education, programming, and technology. The club organizes coding competitions, hackathons, workshops, and technical seminars to enhance students' programming skills and keep them updated with the latest technology trends.

Achievements

  • Best ACM Student Chapter Award 2023
  • Successfully conducted 'CodeCrush' hackathon with 1000+ participants
  • Published research papers in collaboration with industry experts
  • Organized international webinar series with speakers from Google, Microsoft, and Amazon
